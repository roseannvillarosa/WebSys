    These are the different semantic tags added on HTML 5.

            <header></header>

            Aside means "other than that" or "aside from that", usually used for side navigation
            <aside></aside>

            Contains the main content of the page, tells what the page all about.
            <main></main>
            
            <footer></footer>
            <section></section>
